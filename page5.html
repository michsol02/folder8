Empowering Supporters

Responsible 100 enables campaign groups to raise their issues right up to the top of boardroom agendas in businesses right around the world.

We have a proud record of bringing those holding corporate power to account together with the very businesses they scrutinise. Supporter organisations are integral to our approach and model, and to identifying and influencing positive change in business.

Open-sourced, co-created resources

Through an open and collaborative process, Responsible 100 creates, continually improves and deploys unique responsibility benchmarks. We work with a diverse range of organisations, together developing increasingly valuable resources with which to scrutinise and assess businesses, and influence positive change.

Injecting a diversity of thought

No responsibility issue can be properly understood without exploring the diverse sources of expertise, knowledge and experience across civil society. Responsible 100 brings a uniquely broad group of campaigners and experts together to inject a diversity of thought and of perspectives in shaping best responsible business practices.

Real access & influence

Responsible 100 creates unique opportunities to examine and shape behaviours, culture and impacts in and around business. We work with our "critical friends" inside businesses. We enable campaign groups to raise their issues right up to the top of boardroom agendas in businesses, in all four corners of the world, and keep them there.

Catalysing a race to the top in business

Responsible 100 has created new ways to hold businesses to account, and to assist them in identifying better ways of operating. NGOs have an increasing role to play in finding paths for businesses seeking to help build and be part of a fair, sustainable and livable economy. By enabling real responsibility to drive business success, we can release enormous potential to improve our world.

"Corporate reponsibility efforts are often regarded as greenwash because they fail to explore and reveal what really happens on the ground - Responsible 100 identifies and reflects reality "
Head of International Development Policy , WWF

HOW IT WORKS

Responsible 100 is a management tool and a growing community for all those passionate about harnessing the power of business to improve our world.

Through a managed process within a safe and supportive space, we're charting current business performance on a variety of critical social, environmental and ethical issues.
