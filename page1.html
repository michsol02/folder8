HOW IT WORKS
Combining the conscience of civil society with the innovation and problem-solving skills of business

Simple steps for any business

Responsible 100 maps out the possible pathways for achieving better social and environmental practices. We take any business through simple steps, assessing progress against open-sourced performance benchmarks.

1.
Consider a range of social, environmental and ethical issues

2.
Determine which issues are highest priority for your business

3.
Assess your current performance on those issues

4.
Decide where changes and/or resources are required most, set targets to improve

5. Seek additional support as required
Call on the team at Responsible 100, on our preferred specialist consultancies, and on experts from the widest range of NGOs, campaign groups and think tanks that we work with.

6. Measure & report on progress
Responsible 100 helps organisations to measure and monitor progress, and to effectively communicate the things they are doing well, and the areas in which they are improving. 

7. Assess an ever wider range of issues
Once the highest priority issues are identified, assessed and managed, then go on to explore an ever broader range of issues and discover the accompanying opportunities for further innovation and improvement.
